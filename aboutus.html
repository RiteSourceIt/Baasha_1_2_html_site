About us

Baasha.net is a language learning website for kids created by a mom with goals to make the learning process fun. Baasha.net have been serving the purpose since 2012. We have improve the contents based on the reviews and inputs from our young users.

Our Goal:

Our goal is making language learning a fun for kids. We hope to achieve this goal by creating fun-filled activities, games, songs and stories. It eases the task for parents and all they need to do is introduce baasha.net to their kids.

The best way to learn the language

There are many interactive worksheets to improve letters and word skills. Our small lessons and worksheets are effective. This website will be updated periodically with more videos,printout and activities. You can access them at your convienient time.

Languages we cover

Baasha.net started with lessons and activities for tamil language. Now we have expanded the lessons and activities to Hindi and Kannada too. visit our language page for more details.

Are you looking for a specific activity?

If there is any specific activity you like to do more, please contact using the Feedback & Support option in the top. we will make more worksheets available.
